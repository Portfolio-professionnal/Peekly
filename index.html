Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Peekly platform, project testing, and rewards.

Is it free?

Peekly is 100% free for testers. You can sign up, test projects, and receive rewards without any fees. For project creators, we offer different plans tailored to your needs, starting at €49 per project.

How much do I earn per test?

Rewards vary depending on the projects, typically between €5 and €50 per test. Some projects also offer invitations to exclusive events, recruitment opportunities, or early access to products.

How are testers selected?

Testers are selected based on their profile and criteria defined by project creators. Your interests, experience, and the quality of your previous feedback are taken into account.

How long does a test take on average?

The average duration of a test is 10 to 15 minutes. Some more complex tests may take up to 30 minutes, but they are generally better paid.

How do I receive my rewards?

Payments are made via PayPal or bank transfer once you've reached a minimum of €20. Other types of rewards (invitations, early access) are sent directly to your email.
